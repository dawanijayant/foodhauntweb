About Saddacampus

  • Keeping the bizarre interest of the young astute Indian college student with his new approach to life in mind, a few young individuals thought it prudent to think out of the box, beyond their academic teachings to set up a flagship brand termed Saddacampus.
  • Saddacampus as a platform speaks out its mind in form of quality service assurance in the domains of food delivery as part of its foray into online service vending to begin with.
  • Our Philosophy: Saddacampus is perspective. Saddacampus believes that for every college student his life revolves around his college campus in an overly dependent manner. From food to his apparels to the daily grocery required the life of a student demands a lot of day to day services. Saddacampus is an effort to bridge the gap between the student and the availability of the service to his doorstep.
  • Our Creativity: We are a team of creative individuals each one of us is young, working real hard towards our dreams. Our creativity will be evident to everyone in a short span.
  • Our Vision: To end the lack of services in all the college campuses no matter how remotely they are located . We will one day, it's just a matter of time now.
  • Saddacampus Food Delivery: Staying in the room for lunch or dinner? Tired of the Dine In services? Have a serious grudge against the mess food? No more wondering what is available near you. saddacampus has online menus from the awe-inspiring selection of restaurants around you. Simply select your campus and search for restaurants and cuisines. The restaurant index also includes address and delivery hours. Food delivery service has never been made that easier for campuses!
